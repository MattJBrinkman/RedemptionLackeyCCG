Are Unit Studies Right for Us?

Using a unit study based approach has many advantages for some families. Here are a few questions that will help you decide if unit studies may be right for you and your family.

  • Does your family have (and are they willing to spend) the time (and other resources) necessary for the outside preparation and pre-planning required for success at unit studies?

  • Does your family have a "large picture" thinker who is willing to ensure that each of the educational areas are covered sufficiently?

  • Does your family have a "details oriented" person who is willing to plan out each of the individual units in depth?

  • Are your children excited by both the idea and the reality of undertaking a number of hands on projects?

  • Do your children prefer spending a large amount of time working in depth on a single topic?

An ideal unit study family should be able to answer "Yes!" to each of the questions above. If that includes you, you should investigate unit studies further.
