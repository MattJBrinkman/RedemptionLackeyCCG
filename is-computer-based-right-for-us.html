Is Computer-Based Education Right for Us?

Computer-based homeschool curriculum packages can be an excellent fit for many families. Here are a few questions to help you decide if a computer-based curriculum package is right for you and your family.

  • Is your family new to home schooling or unsure of what homeschooling entails?

  • Do you seek additional assurance that your children are covering all of the required subject areas?

  • Do you wish to limit the amount of time spent in preparation and bookkeeping tasks related to homeschooling?

  • Do your children enjoy spending time on computers?

  • Are you comfortable with your child being on the computer for hours at a time?

If you answered "Yes" to one or more of the questions above, you should investigate a computer-based homeschool curriculum package.
