Is A Traditional Package Right for Us?

Traditional homeschool curriculum packages are an excellent fit for many families. Here are a few questions to help you decide if a traditional curriculum package is right for you and your family.

  • Is your family new to home schooling or unsure of what home schooling entails?


  • Do you seek additional assurance that your children are covering all of the required subject areas>


  • Do you wish to limit the amount of time spent in preparation and bookkeeping tasks related to homeschooling?


  • Do your children benefit from continual review of material covered previously?


  • Does your family want to allow for the possibility of your children transitioning back into a more formal school setting in the future?


      • If you answered "Yes" to one or more of the questions above, you should further investigate a traditional homeschool curriculum package.
