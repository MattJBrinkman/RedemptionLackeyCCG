Homeschool Curriculum Packages: Welcome to the adventure!

You have wonderful children, gifted with imagination, creativity, and unending curiosity. You have been richly blessed, but with great blessing comes the great responsibility of educating each child to maximize his/her potential. If you feel challenged (and slightly overwhelmed) you are in good company.

One of the most important questions you will face is which curriculum to use. Many parents facing this question for the first time feel like saying, "Just tell me what to buy." The primary advantage of homeschooling is that the program is individualized and not a one-size-fits-all institutionalized education.

The purpose of this web site is to help you through the process of selecting a curriculum package for your homeschool. We want to help you find the right homeschool curriculum package; the materials that will help you bring out the best in your children. To that end, we discuss the role of homeschool curriculum packages in the five most common homeschooling approaches:

  1. Traditional text- and workbook based education.

  2. Classical education.

  3. Living books education exemplified by Charlotte Mason.

  4. Unit studies.

  5. Unschooling

We also include a discussion of computer-based homeschooling curriculum and a separate section on math packages.
